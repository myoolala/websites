Differential failed

Date: April 14, 2025
The symptoms

This wasn't a catastrophic failure, it happened over the course of weeks. Namely, the autocross test and tune in April is what finally got us to inspect the "noise" Namely, the differential would make a loud click coming off the line or during turns are low speeds. It was fairly quiet at the beginning of the test and tune but after sliding so much I needed new tires, that did the diff in.

Diagnosing

We put the car on the lift to see if we could make the noise. I would ride the brake while releasing the clutch. My god did it show the issue. The diff would rotate and bind until it snapped back to its normal position. Absolutely wild

What happened

The gears in the mfactory pumpkin, specifically the ones the output shafts splined into, wore into the housing. Probably a mm of material lost, it was gash up bad.

Photo of the damage

What was done

Built a new diff identical to the last. No issues since. 4th diff we have built.
