N54 intake and euro airbox on the US spec N52

Date: Jan 10, 2026
Context

This had been on the list for a long while to do and with all of the kits now available I finally pulled the trigger at least to try it. Not having to worry about DISA valves sounds nice, the torque loss down low didn't look too bad, and my gearing meant I cruised at no less than 3k rpm anyway. It wasn't the cheapest all said and done, but that's mostly for the euro airbox.

Required items

Not much, tortsport and printable auto are your fiend

To actually do the conversion there really isn't much needed. Everything can be bought as a kit now

My thoughts after doing it

Mad I didn't do this sooner

It changed the character of the engine. It wants to be revved more and it pulls harder the more you rev it. There is a bit more intake noise but not much. All in all, worth the $600 to do and I did not do it the cheap way. Grab an intake from a junkyard and don't do the eurobox and it's cheaper than a 3 stage intake if it got new DISA valves.
