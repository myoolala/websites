Oil change, plugs & coils before winter.

Step-by-step guide

Prepare the car and gather tools

Park on level ground, set the parking brake, and chock the rear wheels. Lay out tools, oil, filter, plugs, and coils within reach.

Tip: take a photo of coil wiring before unplugging.

Lift the front and drain the oil

Jack the car, support with stands, remove undertray, place drain pan, and remove drain plug. Allow oil to drain fully.

Replace the oil filter

Remove filter cap, replace filter and O-rings, lubricate new O-rings, reinstall.

Access coils & remove spark plugs

Remove engine cover, unplug coils, pull coils out, and remove plugs using a spark plug socket. Inspect for wear.

Install new plugs & coils

Thread plugs by hand, torque to spec, install new coils, ensure connectors click.

Refill oil & check for leaks

Install new crush washer, torque drain plug, refill oil, start engine, inspect for leaks, reset maintenance reminder.
