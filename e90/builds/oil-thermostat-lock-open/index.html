Oil thermostat lock open

Date: April 06, 2025 Difficulty: easy Estimated time: 15 minutes
Step-by-step guide

Prepare the car and gather tools

Remove the beauty cover the engine and uplug the coolant temporature sensor from next to the thermostat.

Remove the old thermostat

Undo the 2 torx bolts for the thermostat. The thermostat is spring loaded to hold it down while removing the second bolt.

Insert the new thermostat/bypass

Assemble the new thermostat if necessary and insert it into the car.

Note: The spring may change sides. Be sure to install it properly.

Replace the seal

This is probably original to the car, replace it.

Torque down the cover

Thread plugs by hand, torque to spec

Put the beauty cover back on
