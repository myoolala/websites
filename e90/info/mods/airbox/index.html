Airboxes

Overview

What this mod is

The stock 328i airbox isn't bad but for track does have some limitations. There is an added filter in it the Euro cars don't have for gas vapor emissions. In addition, changing the filter itself can be a bit of a pain as the whole box must come out. Not a common issue unless you like rallycross.
Why do it

Benefits & tradeoffs

Depending the airbox, you might get more noise or power or both. It varies. I've seen "cold" air intakes have insane amounts of induction noise.
Planning

What to decide before you buy/install

There isn't much in the way of planning here, I would just be wary of anything claiming to be a cold air intake. The factory intake already pulls from in front of the radiator which doubles as a high pressure feed to help push more air in. Sadly I could not find a good article comparing airbox options.
Options

All for non turbo

Factory Euro airbox
Found on ebay in europe, this is a simple OEM+ option. I had the issue where my VIN claimed this would not work but it definitely did.
Everything else
Honestly there are so many options go on google and do research to what matches your needs the best.
My thoughts on this
I run the euro airbox because I like OEM+ and no complaints. No idea what it did itself as I did it with the n54 intake manifold. I do like the filter design though.
