Headers

Overview

What this mod is

Obligatory please check your local laws to confirm what you are allowed to do as options vary by region
This is the replacement of the restrictive factory exhaust manifold for one that flows more for increased power
Why do it

Benefits & tradeoffs

The factory manifold is a known restriction in the exhaust both for power and sound. From what we've seen, doing the headers alone does increase sound somewhat in the same way running a 135 muffler on a 128 also kind of increases the sound. For context a 135 muffler is a gutted 128 muffler with a slightly larger inlet. Run them together though and the car gains a ton of volume.
Planning

What to decide before you buy/install

There are a few options but ideally if you can do this job with the engine out of the car, your life will be so much easier. In the car sucks. Namely, I recommend to get at least 4 of the deep copper nuts sold by FCP as some of the header options have recesses for the nuts that make tightening them with a wrench difficult.

In addition, get a set of long racheting flex head wrenches. You will NEED them
Header Options

What could you buy

This is what I purchased, no issues when I installed them
Cheap ones
It wouldn't be the first time I ran dirt cheap headers on a car with 0 fitment issues or problems for years on end. Decide your risk level
Catted aftermarket headers
Just look at these prices. Good luck
S54 headers
I've seen a few people claim these bolt right in but have yet to verify this. I personally would like to run this if it's possible
My thoughts on this
If you are going to track the car do it. Adds some sound and with a tune you'll notice a power bump. For my e91 having this, 3 stage intake, and milvs I could keep up with e36 m3's fairly easily on the straights.
