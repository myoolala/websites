Intakes

Overview

What this mod is

The stock 328i intake is just sad and makes most dogs cries after 2 or 3 months of no top end power. It's a simple intake that most N52 cars in the states had from the factory. N51 cars and any x30i car had the much more desirable 3 stage manifold for more power. For power, more flow is the goal.
Why do it

Benefits & tradeoffs

Depending the intake, you may only be trading complexity for power or trading low end torque for high end torque. At minimum, if you have an n52 running a stock 328 or 128 manifold, the 3 stage manifold will only add torque everywhere.
Planning

What to decide before you buy/install

Seals, gaskets, and any special parts. Namely, if you choose to install an intake for top end power, you will need adapter hardware to make it all work. Check out the links
Options

All for non turbo

Factory 328i manifold
No
3 Stage Manifold
This is by far the best all around option. Low end torque with top end power in exchange for 2 DISA valves
N54 Intake Manifold
Trades low end torque for more power. Does require a series of adapters to make work like offset spacers, PCV adapter, and intake adapter
N55 Intake manfiold
Same as the n54 manifold except you will need to block off the DME block from it
22RPD intake
I'm waiting on mine but supposedly this will offer the most power and no sacrifice low end torque either.
My thoughts on this
If you run the stock single stage manifold, replace it. It's a quick job and at worst without a tune you'll make more power. I recommend getting one from a junkyard if possible as they can be pricey now.
