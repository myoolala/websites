M3 Steering Rack

Overview

What this mod is

So you got a fancy 128i on nice coil overs with fat sway bars and now rotates too quick in a slide. Natural solution is faster steering to help you react to mistakes. Thankfully, the m3 steering rack just swaps right in.
Why do it

Benefits & tradeoffs

This is trading off faster steering for using a rack in a method it was clearly not intended for, without servotronic. Specifically, at parking lot speeds the rack is underboosted to the point the steering jutters. On track it's fine but it will definitely be heavy.
Planning

What to decide before you buy/install

This just swaps in, just make sure to find one that works
My thoughts on this
Loved it, would happily do it again.
